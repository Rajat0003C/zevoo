Terms and Conditions

By creating a profile on WorkedWith, you agree to the following:

1. Accurate information

You confirm that the name, age, address, phone number, and identity documents you provide are accurate and belong to you (or, for workers under 18, to your registered profile with parent/guardian consent).

2. Identity verification

Your Aadhaar upload is used only to verify your identity and is not shared publicly with other users. It may be reviewed by our team for safety and trust purposes.

3. Location sharing

If you choose to share your location, it will be used to match you with nearby shops or workers and is visible to the other party only when relevant (e.g., once you apply or hire).

4. Payments

Any pay amounts listed are demands or offers, not guarantees. Actual payment terms are agreed directly between the worker and the shop owner.

5. Respectful conduct

Harassment, discrimination, or fraudulent listings are not tolerated and may result in account removal.

6. Changes

You can edit or delete your profile information at any time from this page.
